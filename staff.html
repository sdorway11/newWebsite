The Tech Academy's Staff

Erik Gross
Co-Founder

Erik Gross is a skilled .NET developer, consultant and mentor with many years of experience in the Portland I.T. community. Erik’s technology career began in the U.S. Navy, as an Electronics Technician First Class. He worked aboard nuclear-powered ships, operating and maintaining the nuclear power plant. He also taught computer theory, electronics, physics, chemistry and more to incoming Nuclear Power trainees. Erik has worked as a manager, business owner, consultant, writer and software developer. With over thirty years of technology work under his belt and a passion for teaching, he is well qualified to train software developers.

Jack Stanley
Co-Founder

Jack Stanley is a young entrepreneur with more almost 15 years of experience in the executive arena. Prior to working at The Tech Academy, he ran two successful small businesses in Portland. He made his way to The Tech Academy as Erik’s top choice for a business partner. Jack has a background in teaching, business consultation, HR, recruitment, public relations, marketing, legal management, personnel, contracts and licenses, quality control, sales and management, and he is certified in several areas. With his background in educating and successful experience in running companies, Jack complements Erik’s technical skill.
